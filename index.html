About Us

Welcome to the Cambridge University India Society website.
Here you'll find all the information you need about our upcoming events, including speakers and club nights, as well as details about membership, the society and the committee. CU India Society is open to all students, regardless of race or religion; all you need is an interest in all things Indian! From Hindi language classes, to fascinating guest speakers, we have something for everyone.

Join Us!

Become a member of CU India Society today! As a member, you will receive discounts and priority tickets to many of our events including the Annual Ball, garden party, club nights and more.
